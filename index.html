    Dogs: Man's Best Friend

    This website explores dog trends overall and specifically in New York City by analyzing five comprehensive datasets: NYC dog licensing records, dog bite incidents, popular dog names, adoption statistics, and breed characteristics. Using interactive Plotly visualizations including scatter plots, bar charts, heatmaps, pie charts, and geographical maps, we explore questions ranging from naming trends and breed popularity to bite incident patterns and adoption dynamics. Key insights reveal the relationship between breeds and bite incidents, identify which dog age groups find homes most quickly, uncover geographical patterns in breed origins worldwide, and compare NYC's canine preferences to overall trends. The analysis also examines how factors like special needs status, breed traits, and seasonal patterns influence adoption rates and public safety incidents, providing a data-driven perspective on urban pet ownership and breed behavior.
